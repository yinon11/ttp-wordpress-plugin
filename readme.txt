== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Is there a free version? =

Yes. Talk To PC includes a **free monthly allowance of 30 minutes (0.5 hour)** of voice conversations with limited features.  
You can upgrade at any time if you need more usage or advanced options.

---

= How does the AI know my website content? =

After you connect, the plugin scans your pages, posts, and products to build a knowledge base.  
You can regenerate the prompt at any time from the settings.

---

= What languages are supported? =

Over 40 languages, including English, Hebrew, Spanish, French, German, Arabic, Chinese, Japanese, and more.

Yes. Products, prices, and descriptions are automatically included.
